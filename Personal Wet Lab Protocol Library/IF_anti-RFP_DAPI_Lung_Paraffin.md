### 3. Blocking

1. Rinse slides in 1X PBS, 2 times, 2 min each.
2. Prepare black box with moist paper and Aqua dest. to keep humidity.
3. Dry slide edges briefly, circle sample with histology pen.
4. Place slides in box, add **100‚ÄØ¬µl blocking buffer** per sample.
5. Incubate at room temperature (RT) for at least 1 h.

### 4. Primary Antibody Incubation

1. Wash slides with 1X PBS, 3 times, 2 min each.
2. Dilute primary antibody with Antibody Diluent (AD) (e.g., 1:50 to 1:500).
3. Add **100‚Äì150‚ÄØ¬µl** diluted antibody per sample.

   Add **100‚Äì150‚ÄØ¬µl** per sample, incubate **60 min at RT**.
